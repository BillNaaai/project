Return Eligibility

Return policies are determined by each individual seller. However, buyers and sellers are encouraged to agree on the following conditions before finalizing a purchase:

  • The item should ideally be returned in the same condition it was received.
  • Clear communication between buyer and seller is required to confirm a return agreement.
  • Return requests should be made within a reasonable period—30 days is a common standard.
  • Digital goods, personalized items, or perishable products may not be eligible for return.

How to Initiate a Return

All returns must be coordinated directly between the buyer and seller. We recommend following these steps:

  1. Contact the seller using the email address provided in the listing.
  2. Explain the reason for the return and include any relevant photos if needed.
  3. Agree on the return method, address, and whether the buyer or seller will cover shipping.
  4. Once approved, securely repackage the item and send it via a trusted courier.

Note: E-Shop does not facilitate or reimburse return shipping fees. All logistics must be handled privately between users.

Refund Policy

Refunds are not issued by E-Shop. They must be arranged directly between the buyer and seller. It is recommended that users agree to the following principles in advance:

  • Refunds should only be issued once the returned item is received and verified by the seller.
  • Partial refunds may apply if the item is returned damaged or used.
  • Buyers and sellers are encouraged to use clear payment methods and retain proof of transfer or shipment.

Exchanges

All exchanges must be organized between the buyer and seller. If the buyer is not satisfied with an item and the seller agrees, the buyer may return the product and request a replacement. In such cases, both parties must clearly communicate expectations regarding shipping fees and product conditions.

Non-Returnable Items

Because return policies are seller-defined, the following items are typically considered non-returnable. Always confirm with the seller before purchasing:

  • Gift cards or digital products
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Opened health, beauty, or hygiene products
  • Items listed as “final sale” or “as-is”
