Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase under the following conditions:

  • The item must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required (e.g., order confirmation email).
  • Items must be returned with all accessories and manuals included.
  • Final sale items and digital products are non-returnable.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team via support@eshop.com or call +27 21 551 5500.
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
  3. You will receive a return authorization and shipping instructions.
  4. Pack the item securely and ship it to the provided address.

Note: Customers are responsible for return shipping fees unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Refund Policy

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and process your refund within **5-7 business days**:

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable.
  • If an item is returned in a damaged condition, a partial refund may be issued.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the return process and place a new order.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Gift cards and digital downloads
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Health and hygiene products (e.g., opened cosmetics, face masks)
  • Final sale or clearance items
