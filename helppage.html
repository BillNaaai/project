Orders & Contacting Sellers

After adding an item to your cart and completing the purchase on E-Shop, the next step is to reach out to the seller directly using their email address found on the item page or order confirmation. Sellers are responsible for confirming orders, discussing pricing, and arranging delivery.

Please keep your order ID for reference when communicating with sellers.

Sellers are responsible for arranging and communicating shipping options with buyers. E-Shop does not handle shipping directly. We advise both parties to agree on terms before completing the transaction offline.

All cancellations, returns, and refunds must be discussed directly with the seller. E-Shop does not mediate disputes. We recommend using traceable communication like email and keeping all correspondence.

Account Help

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Click on "Account" in the navigation bar
  2. Select "Register" from the dropdown menu
  3. Fill in your details in the registration form
  4. Click "Create Account"

You'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link in the email to verify your account.

You can also create an account during checkout if you don't have one already.

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. Click on "Account" in the navigation bar
  2. Select "Login" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click on "Forgot Password?" below the login form
  4. Enter your email address and click "Reset Password"
  5. Check your email for a password reset link
  6. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

The password reset link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't see the email, please check your spam folder.

To update your personal information:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Account" in the navigation bar
  3. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  4. Click "Edit" next to the information you want to update
  5. Make your changes and click "Save"

You can update your name, email address, phone number, and shipping addresses from your profile page.
