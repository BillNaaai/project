Shipping Process

As a consumer-to-consumer marketplace, E-Shop does not provide direct shipping services. All shipping arrangements are managed privately between the buyer and the seller. Once a purchase is agreed upon, the seller will provide shipping options and costs, and the two parties must communicate to finalize delivery terms.

Delivery Arrangements

Delivery times and costs will vary depending on the seller's location, the buyer's region, and the method of delivery chosen. Sellers are encouraged to be transparent and communicate estimated timeframes and pricing upfront. Below is a general guide you may expect, but each transaction may differ:

Region Typical Economy Rate Faster Options Same/Next Day
South Africa Negotiable Depends on courier service Subject to seller availability
Africa Discuss with seller Courier rates apply Not commonly offered
International Seller discretion Must be arranged directly Rare/Not available

International Deals

International shipping is solely dependent on the seller's willingness and ability to ship across borders. If you are considering an international purchase, ensure the seller is comfortable shipping to your country and clearly outlines any additional costs, duties, or restrictions involved.

Shipping Issues

As E-Shop is not involved in the shipping process, any issues such as delays, damage, or lost parcels must be resolved between the buyer and seller. We recommend maintaining clear communication and keeping proof of dispatch (e.g., tracking numbers or courier receipts) where applicable. For item-related concerns, please refer to our Return Policy.
