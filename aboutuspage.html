About E-Shop

Your trusted partner in C-2-C online shopping since 2018

Our Story

E-Shop was founded in 2018 with a clear and empowering mission: to create an online marketplace where everyday individuals could connect, communicate, and trade directly with one another. What began as a modest initiative in a Cape Town garage has since evolved into one of South Africa’s most trusted platforms for person-to-person commerce.

Our journey has been fueled by innovation, resilience, and an unwavering belief in community-driven exchange. Over the years, we've transformed from a small listing hub for gadgets into a thriving space offering thousands of unique items across diverse categories. While we don’t handle payments or shipping ourselves, our platform facilitates meaningful connections—placing the power of commerce in the hands of the people, where it belongs.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Empowering Quality

While we don't verify products ourselves, we provide tools and reporting features that empower users to uphold quality through transparent ratings and feedback.

Community Driven

Our platform thrives on people helping people. From item listings to private transactions, our focus is on building trust and value among everyday users.

Sustainable Exchange

By promoting resale and reuse, we support a circular economy that reduces waste and extends the life of valuable goods through direct person-to-person commerce.

Our Milestones

2018

E-Shop was founded in Cape Town as a community-led marketplace, enabling individuals to list and discover secondhand electronics directly from other users.

2020

Expanded to support a broader range of product categories, including clothing, home goods, and collectibles—fueling person-to-person commerce in new ways.

2022

Introduced profile ratings and buyer-seller messaging tools to enhance trust and transparency in peer-to-peer transactions.

2026

We plan to launch our mobile app, in order to make it even easier for buyers and sellers to connect, negotiate, and manage listings on the go.
