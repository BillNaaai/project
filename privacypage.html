1. Overview

Welcome to E-Shop. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle the data you provide when using our peer-to-peer marketplace, and how we empower you to control your information.

This policy applies to all personal information collected through our platform and related services that enable direct communication between buyers and sellers.

2. Information We Collect

We collect certain information to facilitate safe and transparent interactions between users:

2.1 Personal Information

To use our platform, we may ask you to provide personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City and region for listing location

Note: Payment information is not handled by E-Shop. All transactions are arranged directly between users.

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use the platform (e.g., which pages you view, your IP address, browser type, and session activity) to improve functionality and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to support the user experience and improve platform functionality. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Maintain platform functionality and security
  • Provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • Display your listings and profile information
  • Enable communication between buyers and sellers
  • Send platform-related notifications or feature updates
  • Prevent misuse and enforce our community standards

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your information may be shared in limited ways:

4.1 With Other Users

Your email and contact details are visible to other users on your listings to facilitate direct communication regarding sales.

4.2 With Service Providers

We may use third-party tools for hosting, analytics, and customer support. These providers have limited access and must safeguard your data.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if legally required to do so, or if necessary to protect platform integrity or user safety.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking tools to enhance your experience on the site:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain session state during browsing
  • Preference Cookies: Save your preferences for future visits
  • Security Cookies: Help protect against misuse or fraud
  • Analytics Cookies: Provide insight into user behavior for platform improvement

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data. However, as no system is entirely secure, we encourage you to use strong passwords and be cautious when sharing personal details with other users.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to support your account and meet legal obligations. You may request to delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • View or update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications
  • Restrict or object to certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, please email us using the contact information below.

9. Children's Privacy

This platform is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered without consent, please contact us to remove the account.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing the platform from outside South Africa, please note that your data may be processed and stored in jurisdictions with different privacy laws. By using the platform, you consent to this processing.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as our platform evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the platform indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
